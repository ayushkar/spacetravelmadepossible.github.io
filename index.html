services offered

The vision is to open space flight and the space frontier to private citizens by arranging flights to space for more people than have made the journey since the dawn of the Space Age,our aim is to Expand humanity’s reach into deep space

The aim is to further the exploration and settlement of the planet through public outreach, support for government-funded research and exploration, and private-enterprise exploration and settlement

The mission is to bring about radical breakthroughs for the benefit of humanity, thereby inspiring the formation of new industries and the revitalization of markets that are currently stuck due to existing failures or a commonly held belief that a solution is not possible.

the concept of space tourism is one of the most exciting emerging features of the wider tourism industry ,and the companies like virgin galactic and spaceX are already making waves by outlining plans to deliever various forms of commercial space flight in the near future.

we want as many people as possible to experience what it is like to live in space, to circle the Earth, or travel beyond Earth orbit. In the next ten years, our clients will have a choice as to what vehicle to fly to space on …
